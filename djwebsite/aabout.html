About Me: Where the Music Started

Hiram is a Houston-based DJ and the owner of a growing DJ agency, built on a lifelong passion for rhythm and energy. Following in the footsteps of veterans who have spent decades perfecting the craft, Hiram’s journey began with a simple mission: to turn any gathering into an unforgettable celebration.

With years of experience behind the decks, he has rocked everything from high-energy bar nights and intimate weddings to large-scale corporate events at venues like BMC and the George R. Brown Convention Center. Like the most trusted names in the industry, Hiram understands that a great event is about more than just playing songs—it's about professional sound, smooth transitions, and reading the crowd to keep the floor jumping.

Our primary objective is to provide exceptional service and professional vibes every time you step onto the dance floor. Whether it’s a laid-back lounge set or a packed celebration, we focus on making memories that last long after the final track ends.
