About Me

Hiram is a Houston-based DJ and owner of a growing DJ agency known for bringing energy and versatility to every event. With years of experience behind the decks, he’s rocked everything from open-format bar nights and weddings to high-profile corporate events at venues like BMC and the George R. Brown Convention Center. Whether it's a laid-back lounge set, a packed dance floor, or a corporate celebration that needs the right vibe, Hiram blends genres seamlessly and knows how to read any crowd. His focus is simple — deliver professional sound, smooth transitions, and unforgettable moments every time.
